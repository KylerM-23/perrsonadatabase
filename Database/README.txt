The program allows for the user to choose to view the differnt members of each team, those who have the power of the Wildcard, 
or view the people who belong to a specific arcana. Information such as Name, Image, and other info is stored in the database and
is recovered to be used. The general theme song for each game is played when you select a certain team and another is played when 
you choose Wildcard or Arcana.
